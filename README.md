##  BOQ Preparation & Costing Approach

The BOQ analysis follows a structured and professional procurement methodology:

### 1. Engineering BOQ Review
- Study BOQ line items and technical descriptions
- Identify material requirements by construction phase
- Validate quantities for completeness and consistency

### 2. Quantity Breakdown
- Translate BOQ quantities into measurable units (sqm, m³, tonnes, units)
- Group materials into logical categories:
  - Site preparation
  - Foundation works
  - Superstructure
  - Finishing
  - MEP (Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing)

### 3. Market Price Analysis
- Collect **minimum 3 vendor quotations** per major item
- Benchmark prices against current market rates
- Identify price variations and negotiation opportunities

### 4. Vendor Selection
- Evaluate suppliers based on:
  - Cost competitiveness
  - Delivery reliability and timeline
  - Material quality and compliance standards

### 5. Cost Build-up
- Calculate extended costs (Quantity × Unit Rate)
- Aggregate into section totals and overall project total
- Apply 10% contingency for risk coverage
